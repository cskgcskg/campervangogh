Questions

Frequently Asked

How much does it cost to hire Vincent?

Hire is £130 per day with a refundable £750 security deposit. Comprehensive insurance, 24/7 breakdown cover, unlimited mileage and camping furniture are all included in the daily rate.

Where is Camper Van Gogh based?

We are based in Hampshire, UK. Vincent is collected from and returned to our Hampshire location.

Are pets allowed in the campervan?

Yes — pets are fully welcome. Adventure is better together, and that includes four-legged co-pilots. There are no extra fees or restrictions for bringing your dog.

How many people can sleep in the campervan?

Vincent sleeps four adults. The pop-top roof opens to reveal a spacious upper bed, and the rock'n'roll lower bed converts from lounge seating to a second double bed.

Is there a mileage limit?

No. Unlimited mileage is included, so you are free to take Vincent as far as you'd like — including Scotland, Wales, or anywhere in the UK.

What kind of campervan is Vincent?

Vincent is a 2022 Volkswagen Transporter T6.1 with an automatic DSG gearbox. The interior is a hand-crafted conversion featuring leather captain's seats, a two-burner stove, fridge, oven, sink, solar panels and a leisure battery for full off-grid capability.
